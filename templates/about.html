About Sinhala Fingerspelling

A modern web application designed to make learning and teaching Sinhala sign language more accessible and interactive through technology.

Sinhala Language Support

Complete support for Sinhala script with accurate processing of complex vowel combinations, consonant clusters, and special characters.

Video Visualization

High-quality video demonstrations of each fingerspelling sign, automatically sequenced to spell out complete words and phrases.

Smart Conversion

Advanced algorithm that handles Yakaranshaya, Rakaranshaya, and other complex Sinhala linguistic rules for accurate fingerspelling conversion.

Accessibility Focused

Designed with accessibility in mind, making sign language learning more inclusive for students and educators with various needs.

Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices for learning on the go.

Educational Tool

Perfect for teachers, students, and anyone interested in learning or teaching Sinhala sign language in an interactive way.

Fingerspelling Algorithm

Priority 1: Special Combinations

Handles Yakaranshaya (්‍ය) and Rakaranshaya (්‍ර) patterns with optional vowel modifiers. These complex combinations are processed first to ensure accuracy.

Priority 2: Consonant-Vowel Pairs

Identifies consonants followed by vowel modifiers and converts them to separate hal consonant and independent vowel signs.

Priority 3: Hal Combinations

Processes consonants with hal marks and handles consonant clusters with appropriate inherent vowel insertion.

Priority 4: Individual Characters

Handles standalone vowels, consonants, and special characters, ensuring every input character is properly converted.
